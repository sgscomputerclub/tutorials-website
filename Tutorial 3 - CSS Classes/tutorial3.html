Inheritance

This is a div with id="box" and no class.

This is a div with id="box" and class="red".

Due to inheritance, the box's width and height properties are carried over from its "red" class.

This is a div with id="box" and class="long".

Due to inheritance, the box's width and color properties are carried over from its "long" class.

This is a div with id="box" and class="tall".

Due to inheritance, the box's color and height properties are carried over to its from its "tall" class.

Its ok if you don't understand inheritance at this point, but its a useful thing to know about and you can always ask for help.

Classes are defined in CSS. See "style.css" for more info on defining classes.
